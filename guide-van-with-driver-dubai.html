Van with a driver in Dubai for groups.

Quick answerA van with a driver in Dubai is a Mercedes V-class or Vito for 6–7 passengers with luggage and a private chauffeur. Ideal for groups, families and delegations: everyone in one car, at a fixed price. Pricing is sent on request.

A van with a driver is a spacious vehicle with a private chauffeur for groups, families and delegations. Everyone and the luggage travel in one car at a fixed price — more convenient and often better value than splitting the group across several taxis.

Which vans are available

  • Mercedes-Benz V-class — a comfortable business-level cabin for 6–7.
  • Mercedes-Benz Vito — a roomy option for groups and luggage.

What it's for

  • Group transfer — from DXB/DWC, between emirates, to events.
  • Families — with children, strollers and suitcases.
  • Delegations — a team in one car with a driver.
  • Tours — city and out-of-town trips for the whole party.

Why it beats several taxis

The group travels together rather than scattered across cars, the price is known in advance and charged per car. The driver helps with luggage and takes your route with no changes.

FAQ

How many people fit in a van?

Mercedes V-class and Vito seat 6–7 passengers with luggage. For larger groups we can provide several vehicles.

Is it suitable for a family with children and luggage?

Yes. The spacious cabin and boot suit families with suitcases and strollers. Child seats can be arranged at booking.

Can I get a van for an airport transfer?

Yes. A van with a driver is ideal for a group transfer from DXB/DWC: all passengers and luggage in one car, at a fixed price.

Is the price per car or per person?

Per car within its capacity, not per passenger — often better value than several taxis.

How do I book a van with a driver?

Message us on WhatsApp with the route or mode, passenger and luggage count and date — we'll confirm and send pricing.
