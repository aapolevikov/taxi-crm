FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer airport transfers from DXB and DWC?
Yes. We provide meet-and-greet at both Dubai International (DXB) and Al Maktoum (DWC) airports, track your flight, and adjust the pickup time if it is delayed at no extra charge.
Can I book a car with a driver by the hour?
Yes. Hourly hire puts a car and a professional chauffeur at your disposal for meetings, shopping or events, usually from a few hours.
Which cars are in your limousine fleet?
Our fleet includes Mercedes S-Class and BMW 7 Series sedans, Cadillac Escalade and GMC Yukon SUVs, and business-class options such as the Lexus ES, all late-model and well maintained.
How do I pay for a limousine booking?
You can pay by card through a secure payment link or in cash to the driver. A receipt is always provided.
Do you serve other emirates besides Dubai?
Yes. We handle intercity transfers across the UAE, including Abu Dhabi and Sharjah, with comfortable cars and experienced drivers.
Can I rent a car with a driver in Dubai monthly?
Yes. We offer monthly car with driver hire in Dubai for individuals and companies — a dedicated chauffeur and vehicle with simple monthly billing. Tell us your usage and we'll quote a rate.
Do you provide a private personal driver in Dubai?
Yes. You can hire a private driver in Dubai for the day or on a regular basis — a professional, licensed chauffeur assigned to you, for business or personal trips.
