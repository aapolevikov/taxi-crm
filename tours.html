FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What transfer services do you offer for tourists?
We provide airport pickups, hotel and city transfers, day trips and sightseeing tours across Dubai and the UAE, with English- and Russian-speaking drivers available.
Do you provide child seats for family transfers?
Yes. Child and booster seats are available on request, just tell us the children's ages when you book.
Can you arrange regular school-run transfers?
Yes. We set up recurring morning and evening school transfers on a fixed weekly schedule, with the same trusted driver where possible.
Which languages do your drivers speak?
Our drivers speak English and Russian, and we can match the language you prefer when arranging your transfer.
How far in advance should I book?
For airport pickups and tours we recommend booking at least a day ahead, though we do our best to accommodate same-day requests.
