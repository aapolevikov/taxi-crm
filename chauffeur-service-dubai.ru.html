FAQ

Chauffeur service in Dubai — your questions.

How much does a chauffeur service in Dubai cost?

It's priced by the hour, by transfer, or as a monthly package. Hourly hire suits meetings and events, fixed-rate transfers suit the airport, and monthly chauffeur packages give you a dedicated driver and car. Share your trip and we'll quote a rate.

Can I hire a private chauffeur by the hour?

Yes — a professional chauffeur and luxury car at your disposal for meetings, shopping, events or a full day, usually from a few hours.

Do you offer airport chauffeur service at DXB?

Yes — meet-and-greet at Dubai International (DXB) and Al Maktoum (DWC), with flight tracking and help with luggage.

Which cars are available with a chauffeur?

Mercedes S-Class and BMW 7 Series sedans, EQS/EQE electric, Cadillac Escalade and GMC Yukon SUVs, and Mercedes V-Class vans — all late-model and chauffeur-driven.

Can I get a monthly chauffeur in Dubai?

Yes — a dedicated professional driver and vehicle for regular business or personal use, with simple monthly billing.
