About Mr Ride

Dubai's modern fleet operator

Mr Ride is a licensed fleet operator based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. We partner with the leading ride-hailing platforms to provide premium taxi service across the emirate.

Our drivers are professional, licensed, and trained to deliver a consistent, high-quality experience. We use modern technology to manage our fleet — ensuring transparency, fair compensation, and reliable service.

  • Licensed by Dubai RTA (Road & Transport Authority)
  • Professional, experienced drivers
  • Modern Mercedes, BMW, and Cadillac vehicles
  • Multi-platform availability for passengers
  • Transparent operations and reporting

Built for scale

We invest in technology to run our fleet efficiently — from real-time driver tracking to automated payroll. This means better service for passengers and fair compensation for drivers.
