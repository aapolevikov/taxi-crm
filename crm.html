FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Ride CRM?
Ride CRM is fleet management software for taxi and limousine companies. It combines GPS tracking, driver management, finance and payroll, per-car profit and loss, and reporting in one system.
Which ride-hailing platforms does it integrate with?
Ride CRM integrates with Yango, Uber, Bolt and Careem, pulling trips, earnings and driver stats into one dashboard.
Can I track my vehicles in real time?
Yes. Ride CRM includes live GPS tracking with trip history, so you can see where every car is and review past routes.
Does it handle driver payments and payroll?
Yes. The finance module manages driver earnings, deductions and payroll, and shows profit and loss per car.
Is Ride CRM available as a hosted web app?
Yes. Ride CRM runs in the browser, so your team can use it from any device without installing software.
