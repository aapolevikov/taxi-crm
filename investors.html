For investors who want a clean, asset-level return. You fund specific vehicles and earn a share of what each one generates.

FAQ

Passive income in Dubai — your questions.

Is this a good passive income investment in Dubai?

It's designed for investors who want passive income from a real, tangible asset. You fund vehicles in a managed Dubai fleet and earn a share of what each one generates, with transparent per-vehicle reporting — a hands-off, asset-backed way to invest in Dubai.

How does this compare to Dubai real estate returns?

Dubai residential property typically yields around 6–8% per year. Our per-vehicle model targets roughly 20% annual cash yield, and partnership packages target higher — while remaining backed by tangible, insured assets, much like real estate. It's positioned as a high-yield alternative to property in Dubai.

Is the investment asset-backed and secure?

Yes. Until full payback, your capital is held as specific vehicles you own, each under full Comprehensive (KASKO) insurance, with residual value of 40–60% of cost after five years. The vehicles are real assets you can value and, if needed, sell to recover capital.

What's the minimum to start investing?

You can start by funding a single vehicle, or scale into a partnership package from 30 cars upwards. Exact amounts, contracts and projections are shared in the full investor deck on request.

Can foreign investors invest from outside the UAE?

Yes. The structure is set up as a UAE company under DIFC or DMCC frameworks, with funds held in a company account under investor oversight. We'll walk you through the legal structure and contracts directly.
