Two ways to invest

Choose the structure that fits you.

Program 01 · Partnership

Stake in the company

For investors who want scale and upside. You take a controlling position and a majority of profits, with the fleet itself as security.

  • Controlling stake of 51% — you can block key decisions.
  • Majority of profit — 100% until your capital is returned, then 50%.
  • Asset-backed — vehicles you own + full Comprehensive insurance.
  • Fleet packages from 30 up to 240 vehicles.
  • Path to scale — roadmap toward IPO by 2030.
Program 02 · Per-vehicle

Own cars in the fleet

For investors who want a clean, asset-level return. You fund specific vehicles and earn a share of what each one generates.

  • Tangible ownership — your capital is specific cars, fully insured.
  • ~20% target cash yield per vehicle, per year.
  • Residual value — 40–60% of cost retained after 5 years.
  • Transparent P&L — revenue and costs tracked per car in our CRM.
  • First payouts ~30 days after a vehicle goes live.

Security & governance

Built to protect the investor.

Legal structure

  • UAE company; registration under DIFC or DMCC frameworks
  • 51% investor stake fixed in founding documents
  • Funds received to a company account, under investor oversight
  • Independent audit available (e.g. PwC / Deloitte)

Asset protection

  • Capital is held as vehicles you own until full payback
  • Full Comprehensive (KASKO) insurance on every car
  • Residual value 40–60% of cost after 5 years
  • Option to sell vehicles and recover capital
