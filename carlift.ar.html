FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can tourists rent a car in Dubai?
Yes. Visitors can rent with a valid passport, visa and either a home-country licence with an International Driving Permit or a licence from an eligible country. We confirm the exact requirements for your nationality before pickup.
What documents do I need to rent a car?
A valid driving licence, an Emirates ID or passport, and a credit card for the security deposit. UAE residents rent with a local licence.
Do you offer daily, weekly and monthly rates?
Yes. We offer daily, weekly and monthly rentals, with a lower per-day price the longer you rent.
Is insurance included in the rental?
Basic insurance is included with every rental. Additional coverage to reduce your excess is available on request.
Is there a security deposit?
Yes. A refundable deposit is held on your credit card for the rental period and released after the car is returned in good condition.
